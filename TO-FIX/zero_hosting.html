Hosting & Cloud

We've teamed up with Fasthosts, the Uk's most respected data centre, to bring you a range of online services, from simple hosting to cloud storage and everything in-between. But it does just end there; we also use Amazon's AWS and Microsoft's Azure platforms to provide you with the most reliable, fully managed hosting solutions.

Zero42's' fully managed solutions are supported by our own dedicated in-house technicians and backed by three of the industry's most revered on-demand cloud computing platforms. All data is stored here in the United Kingdom in facilities certified ISO 27001 thats the highest level of security you can get. To keep things green, the UK data centre is also powered by 100% renewable energy provided by Ørsted.

We pride ourselves on offering a range of fully supported services to those with very little or no technical skills, although we will happily work with I.T professionals too.   • Step 1

    Build a fast, fully responsive website from the ground up.

    The site had to handle large amounts of data and about 12-million property records, including photographs, maps, local area information, and sales and rental information.

    We used:
    • Full hosted LAMP stack of Linux, Apache, MySQL, and PHP
    • AWS Lightsail + EC2
    • AWS Security Hub
    • Domain Registration &Management
  • Step 2

    Design and build a mobile phone app and payment gateway with digital wallet.

    The client needed a fully integrated mobile phone app with links to merchants Stripe, Paypal and Crypto. We created a super slick Progressive Web App with API links to all three merchants and later added Google Pay, Apple Pay, and AmazonPay.

    Our client also wanted to issue their own debit cards to their members, and we achieved this using Railsbank, a global embedded finance experience platform.

    We deployed:
    • Digital Wallet & Member Blockchain
    • Payment Gateway Integration
    • Progressive Web App + SaaS
  • Step 3

    Secure customer registration and user management.

    The business already had 230,000+ members but wanted “hard as nails” identity and access management, so we deployed Keycloak with OAuth, which allowed members to securely register personal details, store 2048bit encrypted passwords or quickly sign in with their Google, Linkedin or Facebook profiles (single sign-in).

    Our client wanted to use Hubspot to manage customer relationships; this was not a problem using the fantastic Hubspot API. It all worked out so well we now use Hubspot ourselves.

    We Delivered:
    • Keycloak Server
    • 2048bit Encryption of user data
    • OAuth Single Sign-in
    • SMS / Phone / Email verification
    • Hubspot customer relationship management integration
  • Step 4

    Email marketing campaign

    Following a highly successful advertising campaign on various social media channels, we were presented with the complex task of sending out follow-up marketing to well over 2 million recipients whilst complying fully with the United Kingdoms' complex General Data Protection Regulations. So we built mail-blast, a fully automated marketing platform that handles subscriptions and marketing with tracking, denylist management and reporting, able to accurately send 500,000 emails in around an hour, each personalised and with an entire end to end audit trail.

    We also needed to send authenticated bulk emails at an insanely low price without sacrificing deliverability, quality or compliance

    We created:
    • In house marketing platform with template manager
    • Automated GDPR compliance
    • Subscriber management
    • Fast multi-threaded emails via SES
    • Sending 500,000 marketing emails now costs less than £500. Mailchimp wanted over £5000!
